Jack Black

Jack Black is an actor and the lead singer of the band Tenacious D. He will be speaking about what it is like to travel the world in a famous rock band and why he gravitated towards the guitar.


Josh Homme

Josh Homme is a singer/songwriter and multi-instrumentalist best known for his work with the band Queens of the Stone Age. He will be speaking about the importance of hard work and practice when it comes to playing an instrument as well as how you shouldn't expect to get anything material out of making music. He is a big believer in that you have to make music you like, not because you think others will enjoy it.
