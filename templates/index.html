This conference is for individuals like myself that are novice musicians looking for ways to improve their playing techniques on instruments including the guitar, bass, and drums. During each of our three workshop time slots, there will be a guitar, bass, and drum specific workshop that one may choose to attend. The first timeslot will have more foundational and basic workshops, the second will be more of an intermediate level that builds on the first, and the third and final timeslot will feature special guests including Tom Morello, Flea, and Travis Barker! We look forward to seeing all of you there!
